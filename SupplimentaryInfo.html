Project Analysis and status

Putting a good spin on work is wise, if not expected, in the profession world. However, being with a medical rehab team has taught me that in this situation to be transparent with my progress with Netplay as I recover my capabilities is more important than it otherwise might be. I will therefore openly declare how much I have achieved (or not achieved) and trust that you will see this as a way of identifying anything I might need to work on.

Please remember the steep learning curve at the beginning of this rehabilitation must only be traversed once. Hopefully you will take that into account when you assess what I have (and have not) achieved. The state of my memory only improves with time, and I expect this to continue. My supports are carefully constructed to carry me forward during this process.

Fortunately the new paradigms and principles within the task are now firmly fixed in my mind. I do not have any problems understanding any of it or remembering the new concepts and implementations. The challenge will be the management of the tasks. By analogy I now understand Special relativity again (from my Physics), but I would have to look up how to mathematically integrate using 4-vectors several times before it sticks.

Unfortunately to cut a long story short, I have to rate myself at only about
5 out ot 10
. Almost all of the time has been spend on researching, and trying the technologies mentioned in the brief. I do not how much I knew before the injury, but everything was new to me now, so I needed to read up and try out everything. This will not need to happen again.

Please know that normally I would see the delivery of the work with all the bells and whistles on time as of primary importance. However,this for me this as not typical situation. For this first exposure to the new technologies, I have been doing the research and trying the technologies mentioned in the brief for almost all the time given. (Also I have found online many time saving tools to help me in the future).

Please bare in mind that I will not need to do this again.

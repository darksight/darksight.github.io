Project Analysis and status

Putting a good spin on work is wise, if not expected, in the profession world. However, being with a medical rehab team has taught me that in this situation to be transparent with my progress with Netplay as I recover my capabilities is more important than it otherwise might be. I will therefore openly declare how much I have achieved (or not achieved) and trust that you will see this as a way of identifying anything I might need to brush up on.

Fortunately the new paradigms and principles are now firmly fixed in my mind. I do not have any problems understanding any of it or remembering the new concepts and implementations. The challenge will be the management of the tasks. By analogy I now understand Special relativity again (from my Physics), but I would have to look up how integrate using 4-vectors several times before it sticks.

Unfortunately to cut a long story short, I have to rate myself at only about 5 out ot 10. Almost all of the time has been spend on researching, and trying the technologies mentioned in the brief. I do not how much I knew before the injury, but everything was new to me, so I needed to read up and try out everything. This will not need to happen again.

Please know that normally I would see the delivery of the work with all the bells and whistles on time as of primary importance. For this first exposure to the new technologies, I have been doing the research and trying the technologies mentioned in the brief, learning new techniques for almost all the time given. I have found online many time saving tools to help me in the future.

Please bare in mind that I will not need to do this again.
